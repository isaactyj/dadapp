How Support Is Found

The tool looks at recent price lows over the past several months, clusters them together, and picks the nearest price floor below the current price.

How Take-Profit Is Found

Nearby price ceilings (resistance levels) are used first. If price has already passed them, the tool estimates a target based on how far price is from the support floor.
